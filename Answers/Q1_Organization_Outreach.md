---

## 1) Why These Organizations Are Relevant
These organizations represent the four core pillars of our target demographic in Davao: smallholder farmers, coastal fisherfolk, micro-merchants/market vendors, and low-income community borrowers. They possess deep local trust, physical member registries, and established operational networks, making them ideal channels to pilot our financial health and parametric risk solutions.

## 2) How We Plan to Approach and Engage Them
We will seek introductions through the Davao City Cooperative Development Office (CDO) and local Barangay fisheries committees to establish credibility. We will approach leadership with a non-invasive proposal: conducting a joint needs-assessment workshop. Here, we will demonstrate how our AI-led credit scoring, parametric micro-insurance triggers, and voice-ledger tools can decrease their default rates and reduce operational workloads for their field officers.
