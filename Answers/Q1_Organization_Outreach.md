## 1) Why These Organizations Are Relevant
These organizations represent the exact three pillars of our target demographic in Davao: smallholder agricultural producers, micro-retailers/market vendors, and low-income community borrowers. They possess deep local trust, physical member databases, and established operational networks, making them ideal channels to integrate our financial health solutions.

## 2) How We Plan to Approach and Engage Them
We will seek introductions through the Davao City Cooperative Development Office (CDO) to establish official credibility. We will approach their leadership with a non-invasive proposal: conducting a collaborative needs-assessment workshop. Here, we will demonstrate how our AI-led credit scoring and voice-ledger tools can decrease their default rates and reduce operational workloads for their field officers, without requiring proprietary or IP-protected data.
